Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

At Punjab Police B1 TEST App, we value your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and secure your personal data.

1. Data Collection

We collect your Name, Email Address, and Phone Number during registration to authenticate your profile and manage subscriptions securely via Google Firebase Authentication.

2. Payment Security

We do not store your card, netbanking, or UPI details on our servers. All financial transaction data is processed securely through Razorpay, an RBI-authorized payment gateway.

3. Data Protection

Your test history, mock scores, bookmarks, and weak areas are kept highly secure using Google Firebase Firestore security rules and are never shared with any third parties.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact our technical team at: ppb1testapp@protonmail.com
